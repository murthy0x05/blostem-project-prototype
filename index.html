Preface

This project is not merely about building a website.

At its core, RichPerson is designed as a complete, end-to-end intelligent system—one that reimagines how financial guidance should be accessed, understood, and experienced.

The vision extends far beyond a user interface. It encompasses a full-stack architecture that integrates:

  • Speech-to-Text (STT) — capturing user intent naturally through voice
  • LLM-driven reasoning — interpreting, structuring, and generating meaningful financial insights
  • Text-to-Speech (TTS) — delivering responses in a human-like, accessible manner

Together, these components form a seamless conversational pipeline, enabling users to interact with financial knowledge as they would with a trusted advisor.

What makes this particularly significant is that this architecture is not theoretical—it has been independently conceptualized and built as part of BloStem, reflecting a deep focus on system design, integration, and real-world applicability.

This submission represents a foundation of a much larger system—one that prioritizes accessibility, linguistic inclusivity, and intelligent interaction over superficial complexity.

What is my idea?

In a world where financial decisions define your future, understanding them should feel effortless—and personal.

RichPerson offers a curated, intelligent approach to Fixed Deposits, delivered in the language you think in—be it हिन्दी, English, తెలుగు, தமிழ், ಕನ್ನಡ, മലയാളം, বাংলা, मराठी, or ગુજરાતી. Every insight is crafted to be clear, precise, and deeply relevant, eliminating noise and elevating your financial awareness.

This isn't just information—it's perspective.
A refined way to understand, evaluate, and grow your wealth with confidence.

Because true luxury is not just having wealth—it's understanding it.

What I Would Have Built With More Time & Resources?

At the heart of RichPerson is a larger ambition—to evolve from a static platform into a voice-first, intelligent financial companion.

With adequate resources, I would have leveraged the power of Wav2Vec 2.0, specifically the wav2vec2-large-xlsr-300m (a ~300 million parameter Speech-to-Text model), and fine-tuned it extensively for multiple Indian languages.

This would enable:

  • Seamless voice-based interaction — Users could ask financial questions naturally, in their own language
  • Deep multilingual understanding — Supporting diverse accents, dialects, and regional speech patterns
  • Real-time speech-to-insight conversion — Turning spoken queries into precise financial guidance instantly

The result? RichPerson would become not just a platform—but a conversational financial advisor that listens, understands, and responds.

The Only Limitation: Resources

The vision is clear. The architecture is defined. The approach is validated.

The only constraint preventing this from being implemented during the hackathon was access to sufficient computational resources—including high-performance GPUs, large-scale multilingual datasets, and the infrastructure required to fine-tune such a large model efficiently.

In essence:
This is not a question of feasibility—it's a question of scale.

Future Plans

RichPerson is designed to continuously evolve into a frictionless, intelligent financial assistant—where technology fades into the background and clarity takes center stage.

1. Automatic Language Recognition (Zero-Selection Experience)

The next step is to eliminate even the smallest barrier—language selection.

Users won't need to choose whether they're speaking in हिन्दी, தமிழ், తెలుగు, or any other language. Instead, an advanced Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) system will:

  • Detect the spoken language in real-time
  • Seamlessly process the query without manual input
  • Deliver responses instantly in the same language

This creates a zero-friction experience, where users simply speak—and RichPerson understands.
